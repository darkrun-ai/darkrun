# Position

Position kills *ambiguity in the terms*. With the matter framed, the factory specifies the substantive terms the instrument must achieve — the obligations, the consideration, the conditions, the remedies — in language precise enough that two adverse readers cannot in good faith disagree about what was agreed. An ambiguous term is the seed of every contract dispute; this is where it is killed.
