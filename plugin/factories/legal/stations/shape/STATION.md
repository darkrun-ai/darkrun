# Structure

Structure kills the *wrong instrument* — the most expensive structural reversal in legal work. The terms are clear, but the chosen legal vehicle is wrong: a contract where a deed was needed, a single agreement where a master-plus-SOW would scale, a structure that collapses under the tax or jurisdictional reality. Choosing the structure late, after drafting, means a rewrite; Structure pays a cheap proof now to avoid that.
