# Intake

Intake kills the most expensive early mistake in legal work: taking on the *wrong matter*. Before a single clause is drafted, the factory frames what the client actually needs, who the parties are, what outcome counts as success, and what is explicitly out of scope. A matter mis-framed here is a representation that drifts, a conflict discovered too late, or a scope that balloons — all far cheaper to catch now than after the engagement letter is signed.
